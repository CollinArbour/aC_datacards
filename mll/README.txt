Datacards in this directory correspond to samples with up/down histograms produced for all EFT templates for our 4 new nuisances.

Datacards in `nuis_on_sm_only` are our original `mll` datacards that need extra processing with `export_nuis.py` to correct the shapes

Datacards in `DCFix` are the 2018 shapes after being corrected by the `export_nuis.py` script
